Privacy & Security

Your data protection and safety is our priority

1. Data Protection (POPIA Compliance)

We comply with the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA) of South Africa. Your personal information is collected, stored, and processed securely and only for legitimate gym-related services such as bookings, memberships, and communication.

2. SSL Security

Our system is designed to operate over secure connections (HTTPS/SSL) to ensure that all data transmitted between users and the platform is encrypted and protected from unauthorized access.

3. Privacy Policy

We collect basic user information such as name, contact details, and booking history. This information is never sold or shared with third parties. It is used strictly for improving user experience and managing gym services.

4. Terms of Service

By using this platform, users agree to follow gym rules, respect booking policies, and provide accurate information. The gym reserves the right to update services and policies at any time.
